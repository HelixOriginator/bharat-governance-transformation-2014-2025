# Bharat: Governance Transformation 2014-2025

Strategic analysis of India's governance transformation (2014-2025): Digital Public Infrastructure (Aadhaar, UPI, DBT), 500+ scheme reforms, anti-corruption measures, economic growth from $1.86T to $3.94T GDP, and civilizational foundations. PMO-grade policy briefing on India's path to global knowledge superpower.

## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive strategic policy briefing analyzing India's unprecedented governance transformation from 2014 to 2025. The presentation documents the most extensive democratic governance reformation in recorded history, covering institutional, technological, economic, and social domains.

## Key Highlights

### Digital Public Infrastructure
- **Aadhaar**: 1.39 billion enrollments - world's largest biometric ID system
- **UPI**: 13+ billion monthly transactions - world's most advanced real-time payment system
- **DBT**: ₹35+ lakh crore transferred directly to beneficiaries
- **Jan Dhan**: 52+ crore bank accounts for universal financial inclusion

### Governance Reforms
- **500+ Central Government Schemes**: Redesigned with mission-mode execution and saturation coverage
- **PRAGATI System**: PM-level monitoring of 340+ infrastructure projects worth ₹18+ lakh crore
- **Anti-Corruption**: Eliminated 10+ crore fake beneficiaries, saving ₹2.73+ lakh crore

### Economic Transformation
- GDP growth from **$1.86 trillion to $3.94 trillion**
- Rose to **5th largest global economy**
- Ease of Doing Business: Improved from **142nd to 63rd rank**

### Major Schemes
- **Swachh Bharat**: 10+ crore toilets built
- **Ujjwala Yojana**: 10+ crore LPG connections
- **PM Awas Yojana**: 3+ crore houses built
- **Ayushman Bharat**: 55 crore beneficiaries covered
- **Jal Jeevan Mission**: 15+ crore tap water connections
- **PM-KISAN**: 11+ crore farmers receiving direct income support
